Who We Are

The University of Waterloo Energy Network is a group of students bound together by a shared belief in a sustainable approach to the Energy Industry and Climate Change. UWEN offers the chance for like-minded students to attend educational seminars and panel discussions, participate in exciting competitions and network with industry professionals. We endeavor to provide a platform for all points of view with the aim of stimulating discussion and broadening knowledge.
